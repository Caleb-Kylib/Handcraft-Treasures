About Maasai Crafts

Preserving culture, empowering communities, one craft at a time

Our Mission

We bridge the gap between traditional Maasai craftsmanship and the modern world, ensuring that every piece tells a story of cultural heritage while supporting local artisans and their communities.

Founded in 2018, Maasai Crafts began as a small initiative to help local artisans in Narok County showcase their traditional skills to a global audience. What started as a passion project has grown into a movement that celebrates cultural diversity and economic empowerment.

Our Values

Cultural Preservation

We are committed to preserving traditional Maasai craftsmanship and passing these skills to future generations through education and mentorship programs.

Fair Trade

We ensure fair wages and working conditions for all our artisans, providing them with sustainable livelihoods and economic independence.

Sustainability

We use eco-friendly materials and sustainable practices, ensuring our business has a positive impact on both people and the environment.

Community Partnerships

We work closely with local Maasai communities to ensure our initiatives align with their needs and values.

Education Programs

Supporting schools and educational initiatives in Maasai communities.

Healthcare Support

Providing access to healthcare services for artisan families.

Environmental Conservation

Promoting sustainable practices and environmental protection.

Women's Empowerment

Supporting women artisans with training and leadership opportunities.
